A little bit about myself

My name is Andrei. I'm a PhD Candidate and Software Engineer at Ryax Technologies. My PhD thesis is done in collaboration with Ryax, LIG and University of Grenoble-Alpes. The subject of my thesis is upon serverless runtime for hybrid edge-cloud infrastructures. I'm a MSc in Informatics from University of Grenoble Alpes. My advisors are Grégory Mounié and Denis Trystram from Grenoble INP - LIG, and Yiannis Georgiou and Michael Mercier from Ryax Technologies.

I received my M.Sc in Computer Science (Informatic, in French), in 2019 from the University of Grenoble Alpes, in France, where I researched about Scheduling of Jobs in Edgde Platforms, focused on solutions using Smart Heaters.

I did my undergraduate studies at the University of São Paulo, in Brazil. There I started to work on the research area focused on Smart Cities, specifically on AD-HOC and Vehicular Networks. Then, I worked on the same field of my Master, but focused on Data Analyses in real data extracted from Edge Plataforms designed as Smart Heaters. Both workds was done under the supervison of professor Alfredo Goldman.
